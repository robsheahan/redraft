HSC PDHPE — Core 1: Health Priorities in Australia
Analyse how the Ottawa Charter can be used to address cardiovascular disease (CVD) as a health priority in Australia. (8 marks)
Task verb: analyse
What you've done well
  • You've correctly identified all five Ottawa Charter action areas and attempted to apply each one to CVD — this shows you understand the basic framework
  • Your writing is clear and easy to follow, with good paragraph structure that addresses each action area systematically
  • You've made some relevant connections, like linking healthy eating education to developing personal skills
Task verb check — "analyse"

The question asks you to analyse, which means you need to identify components and relationships between the Ottawa Charter and CVD, then draw out implications about how effective these strategies could be.

Looking at your response, you've mostly described what each action area could do rather than analysed it. You need to dig deeper into the "how" and "why" rather than just the "what".

Top priority — focus on this first

Transform your descriptive statements into analytical ones by exploring relationships and implications. For each action area, don't just say what it could do — analyse how it works, why it's effective for CVD specifically, and what this means for different population groups.

Things to improve
  • 1 Add specific Australian statistics about CVD — mortality rates, prevalence, economic burden, and which groups are most affected.
  • 2 Analyse relationships and implications, not just describe. Explore HOW health education leads to behaviour change and what barriers exist.
  • 3 Don't just list examples — analyse how environmental changes remove barriers to healthy choices.
  • 4 Analyse how community-led initiatives create social support networks and reach populations that formal health services might miss.
  • 5 Explore the shift from acute care to prevention and analyse the effectiveness of screening programs versus treatment approaches.
Overall

This response demonstrates basic understanding of the Ottawa Charter framework but sits in the Band 3–4 range because it describes rather than analyses. You've covered the content but haven't shown the deeper thinking that analysis requires.

What a strong response includes
  • Current Australian CVD statistics showing mortality, morbidity, economic burden, and affected groups
  • Analysis of how each Ottawa Charter action area specifically targets CVD risk factors
  • Examination of how strategies work differently for high-risk populations and why
  • Specific Australian examples of policies, programs, or initiatives in practice
  • Analysis of the interconnected nature of the five action areas
