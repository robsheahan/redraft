Feedback. Insight. Action.

Every draft your students write powers all three — one record of how they're tracking, put to work three ways.

For students

Feedback

Marker-voice feedback on every draft — per-line and anchored to your criteria and NESA standards. It shows students what to fix and why, without rewriting their work or predicting a mark.

For teachers

Insights

Every submission builds a picture of how the class and each student is tracking. Spot cohort-wide gaps and watch individual growth over time — the data that tells you what to teach next.

For your classroom

Lesson building

Turn those insights into ready-to-teach lessons. ProofReady targets exactly the gaps your students' work reveals, so your next lesson lands where it's needed most.
